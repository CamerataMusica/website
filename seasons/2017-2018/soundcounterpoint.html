About the Musicians

Established in 2014, sound|counterpoint is charming audiences throughout the Pacific Northwest and beyond with its performances of music from the late Renaissance, Baroque, and 21st century. Based in Seattle, this new ensemble has quickly gained a reputation for performances of expressive generosity, spontaneity, vivacity, and virtuosity. sound|counterpoint’s members play with many of North America’s top ensembles, including Seattle Baroque Orchestra, Portland Baroque Orchestra, American Bach Soloists, Pacific Music Works, and Pacific Baroque Orchestra.

This program is supported in part by a grant from ArtsWa (the Washington State Arts Commission) and the National Endowment for the Arts.
